Pound Cake

Description

Easy pound cake

All you need is ... 4 ingredients

Ingredients

  • 250g softened unsalted butter
  • 250g sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 250g flour
  • baking powder

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 180Â°C. Melt the butter for 30 seconds in the microwave.

  2. Mix the butter with the sugars. Gradually add the eggs.

  3. Once this mixture is done, add the flour little by little. Be careful: do this in small quantities to avoid lumps.

  4. After the flour, add the baking powder. Mix until you have a perfect mixture.

  5. Butter your baking tin, pour in the batter and place in the oven.

    Start watching after 40 minutes of baking. As long as the cake does not blacken, you can leave it in the oven.

    After a maximum of 60 minutes, the cake is sure to be sumptuous.
